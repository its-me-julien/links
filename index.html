About World Mobile Links

World Mobile Links is an independent third-party website that curates publicly available resources about World Mobile. We provide convenient access to external websites, community discussions, and product listings related to wireless and broadband services.

Explore guides on setting up EarthNodes and AirNodes, network documentation, community reviews, and general discussions about the World Mobile ecosystem.

Find information on WMTX staking, network participation, and AirNode-related services.

Whether you're researching mobile or home internet options, or looking for ways to support decentralized networks, we connect you with external sources where you can learn more.

World Mobile Links is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by World Mobile.

What is World Mobile?

World Mobile is a telecom platform that utilizes blockchain technology to expand mobile and broadband connectivity. Through a decentralized, user-owned network approach, it seeks to offer connectivity to everyone, everywhere.

Using a combination of user-owned AirNodes and established telecom partnerships, World Mobile aims to extend connectivity worldwide. For official information about their services and operations, please visit World Mobile’s official website.

World Mobile Links does not provide telecom services, sell AirNodes, or facilitate network operations. Our platform simply helps users find publicly available resources and discussions related to World Mobile’s ecosystem.
